Simon says...
begin the game!

Beginning the game is simple, all you have to do is press the start button located just below the simon game buttons. Once you press start, it will begin the computers turn.

Simon says...
wait for your turn!

Once you have clicked start, the computers will take its turn. You will have to wait for the computer to finish it's turn before beginning your turn. As you progress to level, the game will add another choice to its sequence.

Simon says...
it's your turn!

Once the computer's turn is complete, your turn begins and the computer will wait for you to make your choices. If your choices are complete and correct, the next round will begin.

Simon says...
game over!

If at any point your choices are incorrectthe game will end. If you wish to replay, click reset. If the game ends and you have reached a new high score, the game will ask you for your name and prompt you to submit your score.
